Fix-It

-Fix It is a non-profit initiative that provides healthcare services to the underserved and marginalised areas in Kenya through the help of mobile healthcare units.

Our Services

1. Emergency Care
and Trauma Services

Immediate medical attention for critical injuries and emergencies, including stabilization and rapid response.

2. Maternal and Child Health

Comprehensive care for mothers and children, including prenatal, postnatal, and pediatric services.

3. Surgical Services

Safe and essential surgical procedures performed by qualified professionals in mobile units.

4. Primary Healthcare Services

Preventive, diagnostic, and basic treatment services for common illnesses and health conditions.
